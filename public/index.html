Sacred Soil Collection and Building a Peedam

Collecting soil from sacred places to construct a peedam (a pedestal or sanctum) for a beloved guru is a profound act of devotion. It symbolizes gathering the essence of divinity from various holy sites, uniting them to create a space that embodies spiritual energy and gratitude. This act is not just physical but deeply symbolic, representing the disciple's journey of seeking blessings and channeling divine vibrations into a tangible form.

Gratitude Towards the Guru

Expressing gratitude to a guru is central to such events. The guru is seen as the remover of darkness (gu - darkness, ru - remover) and a guide to enlightenment. The Thirumanthiram highlights the guru's role:

"தெளிவு குருவின் திருமேனி காணல்!
தெளிவு குருவின் திருவார்த்தை கேட்டல்!
தெளிவு குருவின் திருநாமம் சொல்லல்!
தெளிவு குரு உரு சிந்தித்தல்தானே !"
